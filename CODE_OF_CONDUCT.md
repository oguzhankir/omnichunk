# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful environment for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discrimination of any kind
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or ban contributors for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, open a private security-style report with maintainers.
